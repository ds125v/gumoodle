JW FLV Player Back, Light, Front and Screen Color

Colors are defined as hexidecimal values. e.g. black = 000000, white = FFFFFF, red = FF0000, green = 00FF00 and blue = 0000FF

  • Back - control bar background color
  • Light - control bar icons and text color
  • Front - control bar video position scrubber and volume control color
  • Screen - video window background color
